## Overview

The Cybersecurity & Electronic Warfare program gives cadets hands-on experience in how digital systems, devices, and signals are protected in real operational environments. Hosted by Indiana University's Hamilton Lugar School of Global and International Studies and Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering, cadets train alongside university faculty and engineers from Naval Surface Warfare Center (NSWC) Crane.

This is the only program of its kind — designed specifically for Sea Cadets by a major research university and Navy engineers, with training tied directly to real-world Navy missions.

## Courses

### Basic CyberImmersion & Electronic Warfare

No prior cyber experience required — curiosity, motivation, and teamwork are essential. Through team-based, practical challenges, cadets explore how signals are generated, detected, and defended in the electromagnetic spectrum.

**Topics include:**

- Ethical hacking
- Internet of Things (IoT) security
- Data breaches and cloud data protection
- Cybersecurity policy and national security
- Protecting critical infrastructure
- Signal exploration and spectrum fundamentals
- AI/LLM applications

See the [Basic CyberImmersion & Electronic Warfare]({{< ref "/events/EC-IN-2601-Basic-Cybersecurity" >}}) event page for upcoming dates and registration details.

### Advanced CyberImmersion & Electronic Warfare

Designed for cadets with prior experience in cybersecurity, coding, robotics, or other STEM activities. Advanced cadets take ownership of mission execution — designing defensive strategies, analyzing real-time data, and adapting to evolving threat conditions through complex, multi-day mission scenarios.

**Topics include:**

- Network defense and active monitoring
- Adversary simulation and red/blue team exercises
- Secure systems architecture
- Signals analysis and spectrum management
- Cyber mission planning and vulnerability assessment
- Applied electronic warfare problem sets
- Leadership in technical teams and decision-making under ambiguity

See the [Advanced CyberImmersion & Electronic Warfare]({{< ref "/events/EC-IN-2602-Advanced-Cybersecurity" >}}) event page for upcoming dates and registration details.
