## Our Mission

The Sea Cadets Midwest STEM initiative works to expand access to high-quality STEM training for U.S. Naval Sea Cadets in the Midwest region. We connect cadets with hands-on learning experiences in robotics, cybersecurity, engineering, and more.

## Who We Are

We are a network of dedicated volunteers, instructors, and partner organizations committed to building leaders of character through STEM education. Our programs are designed to complement the broader Sea Cadets mission by giving cadets the technical skills and confidence they need to succeed.

## Our Values

Aligned with the U.S. Naval Sea Cadet Corps, we believe in:

- **Honor** — Integrity in everything we do
- **Respect** — For our cadets, volunteers, and communities
- **Commitment** — To excellence in STEM education
- **Service** — To our nation and our young people
