## Greatness Has No Age

The Sea Cadets Midwest STEM initiative brings hands-on STEM training opportunities to cadets across the Midwest region. From robotics and cybersecurity to engineering challenges, our programs help young Americans develop critical skills in a disciplined, team-oriented environment.

Whether you're a cadet looking for your next training opportunity, a volunteer wanting to help shape STEM education, or a partner organization interested in growing STEM capabilities, you've come to the right place.
