### Who can participate in Midwest STEM programs?

All active Sea Cadets and Navy League Cadets are eligible. Some programs may have specific age or grade requirements — check the individual program page for details.

### How do I sign up for a training event?

Find the event on our [Events]({{< ref "/events" >}}) page, note the **training code** (e.g., "ER-IN-2601"), then log in to **Quarterdeck** and search for that code to complete your registration.

### Do I need prior STEM experience?

No! Our programs are designed for all experience levels. Whether you've never touched a robot or you're already coding, there's a place for you.

### What is Quarterdeck?

Quarterdeck is the secure online system used by the Sea Cadets for training registration, records, and administration. All cadets and volunteers have Quarterdeck accounts managed through their units.

### How can my organization partner with Midwest STEM?

We welcome partnerships with organizations that share our commitment to STEM education. Please visit our [Contact]({{< ref "/contact" >}}) page to get in touch.
