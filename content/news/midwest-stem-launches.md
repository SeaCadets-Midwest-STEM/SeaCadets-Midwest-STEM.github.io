We're excited to announce the launch of the Sea Cadets Midwest STEM initiative! Our mission is to expand access to high-quality STEM training for cadets nationwide, with programs hosted in Indiana.

## What to Expect

In the coming months, we'll be rolling out:

- **Regional STEM events** with hands-on training in a variety of STEM related disciplines
- **Curriculum resources** to help cadets and instructors prepare
- **Partnership opportunities** for organizations interested in supporting STEM education

## Get Involved

Visit our [Programs]({{< ref "/programs" >}}) page to learn about available STEM tracks, or check the [Events]({{< ref "/events" >}}) page for upcoming training opportunities.

We look forward to building the next generation of STEM leaders — one cadet at a time.
